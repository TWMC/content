Title: Get Together

----

Text: 

TWMC has a tradition to get together at Hot Staff.
Hot Staff is a friendly and relaxed place. It has been renovated in 2023 and is open for events and our get-together.  If you want to be sure to find someone, please contact one of the committee members.

Now, we have** monthly meeting on  1st Wednesday** of each month from 8:00 pm.

Here is a map for Hot Staff and a bar, Goldrush. The stairs into Hot Staff are on the side street running between the Century21 and the Central 88 Building.
