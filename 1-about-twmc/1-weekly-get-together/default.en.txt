Title: Weekly Get Together

----

Text: 

The TWMC tries to meet every Wednesday from 9:00pm at Hot Staff. Hot Staff is a friendly and relaxed place to hang out, with big tables, good food and plenty of drinks. . If you want to be sure to find someone, please contact one of the committee members.

Here is a map for Hot Staff and another bar, Goldrush. The stairs into Hot Staff are on the sidestreet running between the 7-11 and the Central 88 Building.
