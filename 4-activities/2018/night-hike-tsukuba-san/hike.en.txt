Description: 

Join us for Mt. Tsukuba at night through a different route.

After sunset, we will start hiking from the Tsutsujigaoka (つつじヶ丘) ropeway station and climb to the Nyotai san summit (female peak, 女体山).
Then, we will enjoy the night view from the mountain and take a ropeway to go back to the car parking lot.
During autumn/winter season, "stardust cruising ropeway" is available.
http://www.mt-tsukuba.com/?p=1723

The route and plan is the same as last year's night hike.

Please bring head/hand lamp and warm clothes.
A restaurant is opens until the last ropeway. 
We will have a dinner there.

Difficulty level : 2 out of 5.
Meting time and place : 16:00 at Tsukuba center car parking
