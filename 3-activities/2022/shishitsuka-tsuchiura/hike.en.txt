Description: 

Shishitsuka is a typical Japanese satoyama area in Tsuchiura, not far from Tsukuba.
The Shishitsuka pond is surrounded by a hundred-hectare forest, there are many ancient remains, including the Shishitsuka tumulus and Kamitakatsu shell midden.
