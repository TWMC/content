Description: 

Komachi-yama is located in Tsuchiura and its height is 361m.
Over the last years, the hiking routes have been maintained by local people.
It is a nice relaxing hike, though there a some short steep places.
It will take around an hour to walk up, take lunch at the top. Walking down takes less than an hour.

Date: Sunday, June 11th
Meeting time: 10:00 for cars / some people might go by bicycle and will start earlier.
Meeting place: parking lot next to Tsukuba bus terminal. We will be back to Tsukuba around 15:00.

What to bring: Hiking shoes, gloves, warm clothes, drink and lunch.
