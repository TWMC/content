Description: 

TWMC summer party 2019. A very good opportunity to get together in a casual, friendly and "non-sweating" event. 

**Date and time**
June 22nd, 11:30 to 15:00

**Venue**
Sakura minakaen 2-7-5 Azuma Tsukuba 
https://www.city.tsukuba.lg.jp/shisetsu/bunkagakushu/1002780.html
5 minutes walk from TX tsukuba station.
Japanese Classic style house.

**What to bring**
Potluck style. Recommended, not mandatory, to bring something to eat. If it's something typical from your country, even better!
Beverage, TWMC will offer you like tea, or soft drinks.
***Please note, the venue does not allow the consumption of alcohol.*** 

Please consider bringing reusable plates and chopstics to reduce the amount of garbage. 

Please bring friends and families! The event is open to non-TWMC members.

Report: 

About 30 (!) people got together for the TWMC summer party2019@ sakura minkaen on last Saturday.

Some new members and cute baby members came ^^.
