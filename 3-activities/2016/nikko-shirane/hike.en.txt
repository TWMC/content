This hike was cancelled because of bad weather. 
 
This is the first announcement of one-day Daibosatsurei (大菩薩嶺) hike on 27th Sunday November. (If 26th is fine with you, please let me know.)

Mt. Daibosatsurei is located in Yamanashi prefecture and one of 100 Famous Japanese Mountains.
We will start hiking from Kamihikawa  pass (上日川峠)  and going to climb up to the summit of Mt. Daibosatsurei (2057 m) via Daibosatsu pass (大菩薩峠).
The map time is 4.5 hours.
The difficulty level is 2.5 of 5.

*What to bring:
Hiking shoes
Warm clothes
Rain gear
Lunch box and snacks
Drinks
Onsen stuff
Backpack
 
*Expected Cost:
