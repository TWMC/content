TWMC End-of-year-Party and Photo Contest

1) END-OF-YEAR PARTY
Saturday 10th December, 19:00 - 22:00, Hot Staff bar
Everybody is welcome, bring your friends and family too! No need to register for this, but please bring some food to share. Note that drinks will be provided by Hot Staff, so please DON'T BRING DRINKS.

2) PHOTO CONTEST
I have received photos from some people, but more are welcome. Don't be shy! The deadline is Saturday @ 16:00; any photos received after that will not be considered.

Please send your photos, which must be from this year's TWMC events, to Ovidiu (ovcretzu@yahoo.com) under the following three categories to enter:

A) People - any photo of people or relating to people. Could be TWMC members or not...
B) Nature - think mountains, flowers, animals, sunsets...
C) Fuji-san.
