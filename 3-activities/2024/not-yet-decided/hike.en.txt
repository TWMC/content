This is the first update of the Mt. Takao hike this Sunday, March 10.

The weather outlook is good; it's sunny!

Here are the registered participants. There is a capacity for the car. We can change to the train base hike. So If you are interested, let me know!!

Yunosuke (car, 4)
Anna

Outline of the schedule is below:
5:30 Tsukuba station
8:00 Takaisanguchu station (Start point Mt. Takao)
Climb up by foot or cable car
(if you want to walk on the fire, using a cable car is better)
10:30 Summit of Mt. Takao
Climb down by foot or cable car
13:00 Start the fire festival
There is Plum Blossom Festival near here (We can go if we have time)

Mt. Takao is one of 100 Famous mountains of Kanto. The Fire walk festival will be held on 10th March. 

There is more information here:
Mt. Takao: https://www.japan-guide.com/e/e3029.html
Fire walk festival: https://www.gotokyo.org/en/spot/ev179/index.html

It is a car-based trip. It should take about two and a half hours to reach. We depart Tsukuba at 5:30 AM.

So, if you are interested in joining, please reply to this email and let me know if you have a car (and how many people it can carry).
