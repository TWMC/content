Description: 

The field of the airsoft is hold at Tsukuba Survival Game Land. 20km south from Tsukuba Station. There are many foreigners coming to the battle field.

サバイバルゲーム | つくばサバゲーランド | 日本 (tsukusaba.com)

===Schedule===

Gathering point (for car pooling) from the Tsukuba station temporary (drop-off - pick-up) parking (free for 20 min.) by 7:20 AM.

It will take 30 min to get there. when we arrived the field, we will pay all the fee and rentals, and lunch(if you want) and then prepare the game.

Before the games starting, beginners need to take the instruction and then finally you can join the games.

A game is held approximately 20 min for each and it will be finished by 16:30. There is break and lunch time between games.

You can choose to join each game or not anytime. We will leave the field and back to Tsukuba ST before 18:00, after finished the games.

Cost: 300 yen club fee (exempt for drivers) , gasoline (200～300 yen) and game fee.
