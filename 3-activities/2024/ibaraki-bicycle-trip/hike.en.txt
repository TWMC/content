Description: 

Cycle with me to the most delicious Sri Lankan restaurant in Kanto (スリランカ料理 ラサイ) for lunch on October 19. We’ll also visit my favorite fruit stand for some refreshments on the way back. The route will cover about 56 km through forests and rice and lotus paddies, and near rivers on bike paths, farm roads and lightly trafficked public roads. There will be some small hills but most of the route is flat, with a few short unpaved sections.

The ride will proceed if there is intermittent light rain, but will be postponed in the event of continuous or moderate to heavy rain. Bring water and your bicycle in good working order. Helmets are highly recommended. We’ll ride at whatever pace is comfortable for the slowest participant and won’t leave anyone behind. We should be back by mid-afternoon.

There is some risk associated with cycling and by participating, you agree to accept full responsibility for your own safety and that the organizer and the Tsukuba Walking and Mountaineering Club (TWMC) bear no responsibility for your safety. As with all TWMC day trips, I will collect a 300 yen club fee from each participant.

 Rentals of good bikes are available, but must be reserved at least 3 days in advance:

https://www.ringringroad.com/english/rentalcycle/

https://www.ringringroad.com/english/wp-content/themes/ringringroad/assets/pdf/rental_flyer.pdf

I recommend renting a hybrid (cross) bike or electric bike. I think the rentals come with helmets. Reserve to pick up your bike at 9 am at the Tsukuba Sogo Information Center at the south end of the Tsukuba Station plaza next to Saza Coffee, which is the meeting spot for all participants (36.08189887410655, 140.11271232475767). Please arrive by 9 am.  We will depart at 9:10.

Please share this message with anyone who might be interested.
