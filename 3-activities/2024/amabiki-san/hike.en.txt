Description: 

First announcement for  Amabiki-san hike on Saturday April 13th.
 Amabiki-san is located slightly north of Mt. Tsukuba and is part of the ``Kanto Fureai no Michi''.

This is a car-based trip.
It will be a relatively easy walk of approx 3 hours duration.
We will start from the Iwase Station parking and head for Amabiki temple (Rakuhouji)  through Ontake-san and Amabiki-san this time.
Hopefully we still able to enjoy sakura blooms.

●**What to bring**
- walking shoes
- rain gear if you need
- lunch, water, snacks

● **Meeting point**
- Meeting time is **8:30 am**, NOT 8:00 at the parking lot next to the Tsukuba Center bus terminal.
- cost: 300 yen club fee, share for gas (drivers and children are free)
 
We start walking around 10:00 and have a break for lunch at the top then start walking down to the temple. We can enjoy a beautiful  garden, small animals and peacocks for a while there before heading back to Tsukuba.  If we have more than two cars, driver can pick you up at the parking of the temple. (or by walk /bus to the parking also available depend on the bus schedule)

If you are interested in joining, please send me an e-mail with   your name , your mobile number. If you are coming by car how many people can fit in.

Looking forward to hear from you.
