Title: Japan Alps Update: COVID-19

----

Tags: 

----

Date: 2020-07-29

----

Summary: 

The Hiking in Japan website gives regularly updates about the situation in the Japan Alps for hikers. 
Many huts have reduced capacity, some are closed for the entire duration of the hiking season. 
So visit their website before you make plans to go to the Alps.
